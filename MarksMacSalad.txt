How to make Mark's Mac Salad

1 LB Macaroni
30 Oz Mayo
24 carrots - Use cheese grader to cut carrots.
3 hard boiled egg
salt (~1 tsp) and pepper
Could also add celery.  Don't use a cheese grader to cut it however.

Add 20 oz of mayo the first day along with everything else 
Add 10 0z the second day
